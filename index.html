Introduction

There are many mysteries about our universe. Looking up at the sky- what can we see? More importantly, what can't we see?

In this website, you will learn about one of the phenomenons of the universe - dark matter.

So, what is dark matter?
Dark matter is a form of matter in our universe which does not interact with any other type of matter in space, which is why it is not visible (as it does not interact with light or radiation).

Why is dark matter important?
The study of mysteries like dark matter is important because it helps astronomers understand our universe better. Dark matter itself is distributed across all the universe- it is everywhere.
Understanding dark matter will also provide evidence to unanswered questions regarding universe.
Its study could also depict an idea of what the universe really looked like during its formation, i.e. (The Big Bang) .
Dark matter has been around since the beginning of time itself.
